About Us

PhysioGuardian is a digital platform dedicated to making physiotherapy more accessible and effective. Our goal is to empower individuals on their recovery journey by providing interactive, guided exercises with real-time feedback, a smart routine generator based on user-described symptoms, and a comprehensive clinic locator to connect with professionals.
